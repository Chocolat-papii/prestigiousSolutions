IT Asset Disposal & Recycling (ITAD) in South Africa

Keep your organisation secure, compliant, and sustainable. We help businesses close the loop on the ICT lifecycle with end-to-end IT asset disposal (ITAD) and certified e-waste recycling—aligned to POPIA, GDPR, and leading international best practices.

What We Collect & Process


  • End-User Devices:
    Laptops, desktops, tablets, smartphones, thin clients
  • Data-Bearing Media:
    HDDs, SSDs, tapes, USBs, memory cards
  • Infrastructure:
    Servers, storage arrays, UPS units, network switches, firewalls, Wi-Fi access points
  • Print & Peripherals:
    Printers, scanners, monitors, docking stations, headsets, keyboards
  • Cables & Accessories:
    Power cords, patch leads, adapters, chargers

Our Secure ITAD Process

1. Scope & Asset Audit

We document device types, quantities, asset tags, and data-bearing media, then agree collection dates, access requirements, and chain-of-custody protocols.

2. Secure Collection & Transport

Tamper-evident containers, sealed cages, and tracked vehicles. A signed chain-of-custody accompanies assets from pickup to processing.

3. Certified Data Wiping & Drive Destruction

  • Software Erasure: NIST SP 800-88–aligned and crypto-erase methods for HDD/SSD where appropriate
  • Physical Destruction: Punching, crushing, or shredding of failed or sensitive drives
  • Full Audit Trail: Serial-number logs tied to each data-bearing device

4. Asset Decommissioning

We remove devices from financial, CMDB, MDM, and endpoint security systems to prevent shadow assets and licensing exposure.

5. Reuse, Remarketing & Donations (Optional)

Where policy allows, we test, grade, and refurbish equipment for secondary use to maximise residual value or social impact.

6. Responsible E-Waste Recycling

Non-reusable items are dismantled and recycled through accredited, eco-conscious partners with material recovery reporting.

7. Compliance Reporting

You receive Certificates of Data Erasure/Destruction, e-waste transfer notes, and a complete audit file for internal and external audits.
