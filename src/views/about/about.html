About Us

Company Overview


Prestigious Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a 100% Black youth- and woman-owned B2B technology partner founded in 2024 and headquartered in Johannesburg. We help organisations simplify systems and run smarter with end-to-end ICT lifecycle services - backed by responsive SLAs and rigorous compliance.

Our offerings span ICT Procurement & Licensing (hardware, software, OEM-aligned), Deployment & Implementation (device imaging, configuration, network setup), IT Support & Managed Services (remote/onsite, Microsoft 365), Repairs & Maintenance (with a Samsung-endorsed repair centre in development), IT Asset Disposal & Recycling (POPIA-aligned data sanitisation, certified e-waste), and Office Essentials & Workplace Supplies.

We serve public and private sector clients—including government departments, schools, SMEs, corporates, and NGOs—reducing vendor sprawl, improving uptime, and providing audit-ready documentation. Our value-driven model blends compliance, quality assurance, and speed, so technology becomes simpler, safer, and measurably more effective.
